# Self-Improving Reflections

Purpose: record post-task reflections and reusable workflow lessons while keeping long-form incident detail in daily memory, leaves, topics, or archives.

Records here are candidates by default. They are useful for retrieval and later proposal generation, not automatic hot-memory edits.

## Record Template

```md
---
id: si-YYYY-MM-DD-short-slug
type: reflection
scope: global | project:<name> | domain:<name>
summary: "One sentence that captures the reusable workflow lesson."
keywords:
  - keyword
source_refs:
  - memory/daily/YYYY-MM-DD.md:line-range
confidence: medium
review_state: candidate
verify_before_use: true
valid_until:
last_seen_at:
evidence_count: 1
superseded_by:
created_at: YYYY-MM-DD
last_used_at:
use_count: 0
---

## Trigger

What task, incident, or review produced this reflection.

## Lesson

What should be done differently next time.

## Use When

- When this reflection is relevant.

## Do Not Use When

- Boundary or false-positive cases.
```
